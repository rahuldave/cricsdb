# About me

Hi, I'm Rahul Dave. I built CricsDB because the cricket data that
Cricsheet publishes is rich, free, and almost nobody has the time
to dig around in it at the ball level. Most public sites stop at
innings totals; I wanted somewhere to click through a player's full
career, split every way a fan might care about.

I'm not a professional cricket statistician. I'm a cosmologist by
training, but spend my time doing ML and AI research, teaching,
and consulting these days. You can contact me at
[rahuldave.com/about.html](https://rahuldave.com/about.html) or
on twitter as [`@rahuldave`](https://twitter.com/rahuldave).
Everything on this site is what I personally wanted to look up and
couldn't. If you spot a number that looks wrong, the repo is on
[GitHub](https://github.com/rahuldave/cricsdb) — issues welcome.

— Rahul
