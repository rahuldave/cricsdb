# About me

_This section is edited by the site maintainer. See
`frontend/src/content/about-me.md` — plain markdown, picked up on
the next build._

Hi, I'm Rahul Dave. I built CricsDB because the cricket data that
Cricsheet publishes is rich, free, and almost nobody has the time
to dig around in it at the ball level. Most public sites stop at
innings totals; I wanted somewhere to click through a player's full
career, split every way a fan might care about.

I'm not a professional cricket statistician. I'm a programmer and a
long-time T20 watcher, so everything here is what I personally
wanted to look up and couldn't. If you spot a number that looks
wrong, the repo is on
[GitHub](https://github.com/rahuldave/cricsdb) — issues welcome.

— Rahul
